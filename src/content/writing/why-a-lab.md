---
title: Why a lab, not a portfolio
date: 2026-06-27
tags: [building, ideas]
draft: false
summary: A static portfolio is a snapshot. A lab is a feed — proof-of-work that updates itself.
---

A portfolio says "here's what I did." A lab says "here's what I'm doing, right now." When
an always-on machine publishes its own output, the proof is continuous.
